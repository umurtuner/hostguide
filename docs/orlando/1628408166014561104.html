You'll probably want a rental car. Public transit is limited outside downtown.

Local Tips from Orlando

Theme parksTickets are cheaper on reseller sites (Undercover Tourist, Park Savers).
FoodEat at Disney Springs or Universal CityWalk — no park ticket needed.
OutletsOrlando Premium Outlets (Vineland) has the best deals. Go on weekdays.
WeatherAfternoon thunderstorms daily in summer. They pass in 30 min — just wait it out.

Safety Tips

  • Orlando is generally safe — use common sense
  • Lock your car and don't leave valuables visible
  • Use Uber/Lyft late at night, especially after drinking
  • Afternoon thunderstorms are daily in summer — they pass quickly
  • Use sunscreen — Florida sun is stronger than you think
  • Dial 911 for any emergency
