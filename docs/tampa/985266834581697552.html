Getting Around

Ride apps: Uber and Lyft work everywhere in Tampa.

You'll probably want a rental car. Public transit is limited outside downtown.

Local Tips from Tampa

Ybor CityBest nightlife and Cuban sandwiches. Columbia Restaurant is a must.
BayshoreBayshore Blvd is the world's longest continuous sidewalk. Great for a walk or run.
BeachesClearwater Beach is 30 min west. St. Pete Beach is less crowded and just as nice.
Cuban sandwichTampa invented it. Try La Segunda (oldest Cuban bakery in the US).

Safety Tips

  • Downtown Tampa is generally safe — use common sense
  • Lock your car and don't leave valuables visible
  • Use Uber/Lyft late at night, especially after drinking
  • Afternoon thunderstorms are daily in summer — they pass quickly
  • Use sunscreen — Florida sun is stronger than you think
  • Dial 911 for any emergency
