Local Tips from Dublin

PubsTemple Bar is touristy and expensive. Locals drink in Stoneybatter, Portobello, or Wexford Street.
GuinnessMust try at the source. Storehouse tour is good but any local pub pours it perfectly.
Getting aroundLeap Card works on all buses, trams (Luas), and DART trains. Taxis are expensive.
WeatherRain is constant but brief. Layers + waterproof jacket is the move. No umbrella needed.
Tipping10% at restaurants if service isn't included. Not expected in pubs.

Safety Tips

  • Dublin is generally safe — use common sense at night
  • Avoid walking alone through Phoenix Park after dark
  • O'Connell Street can be rowdy late on weekends
  • Dial 112 or 999 for any emergency
